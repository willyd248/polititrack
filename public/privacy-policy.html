Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

PolitiTrack is a civic data tool that aggregates and presents publicly available information about U.S. legislators, campaign finance, and congressional activity using data from the Congress.gov API and OpenFEC API. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect about our users and how it is handled.

PolitiTrack does not collect, store, or share any data about the political interests or views of its users. By using PolitiTrack, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

CategoryWhat we collectWhy
Usage analytics Pages visited, navigation patterns, session duration, referrer URL (via Vercel Analytics — anonymized) Understanding which features are most useful and improving the product
Technical data Browser type, OS, viewport size, country (aggregated) Compatibility and performance optimization
Error logs Client-side errors and stack traces Diagnosing and fixing technical issues
No personal accounts required: PolitiTrack does not require registration to access civic data. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any identifying information unless you voluntarily contact us.

Public Data Sources

PolitiTrack displays publicly available government data. We do not own or control this data:

  • Congress.gov API: Legislative data including bills, votes, and legislator information. Published by the Library of Congress. See api.congress.gov.
  • OpenFEC API: Federal campaign finance data published by the Federal Election Commission. See api.open.fec.gov.

All legislator profiles, voting records, and campaign finance figures displayed on PolitiTrack are sourced from these public government APIs and are not created by PolitiTrack.

Analytics — Vercel Analytics

PolitiTrack uses Vercel Analytics for privacy-preserving usage measurement. Vercel Analytics:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not build individual user profiles
  • Aggregates data at a country/region level
  • Does not track users across websites
  • Is compliant with GDPR without requiring cookie consent banners

See Vercel's privacy policy at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Cookies

PolitiTrack does not set any tracking or advertising cookies. Vercel Analytics operates without cookies. If you use a browser with cookies enabled, your browser may automatically store functional session data — this is browser behavior, not data we collect or control.

Third-Party Services

  • Vercel: Hosting and serving the application, and providing anonymized analytics. See vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Congress.gov API (Library of Congress): Public legislative data. No personal data is shared with this service.
  • OpenFEC API (Federal Election Commission): Public campaign finance data. No personal data is shared with this service.

Data We Do Not Collect

PolitiTrack is designed to be a privacy-respecting civic tool. We explicitly do not collect:

  • Information about which legislators, candidates, or parties you view
  • Your political affiliation, opinions, or voting behavior
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone)
  • Financial information of any kind

Children's Privacy

PolitiTrack is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we do not require registration, no personal information is collected from any user regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated via notice on the site.
